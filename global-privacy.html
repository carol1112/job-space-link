Global Privacy Policy

Job Space Link respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Global Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification details (name, email, contact information)
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Employment history, resumes, and uploaded documents
  • Communication data between users
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect job seekers with employers
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To communicate updates, notifications, and service messages
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with:

  • Employers or job seekers as part of recruitment activities
  • Service providers supporting platform operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data.

7. International Use

Job Space Link operates globally. By using the platform, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information across international borders.

8. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance platform performance and analytics.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Job Space Link support through the platform.
